### The 9+ Rule
- **9s are RARE** ‚Äî Only for elite tier shows
- **9.5s** ‚Äî Reserved for absolute best-in-category (The Wire world-building, Breaking Bad characters)
- **10s** ‚Äî Nearly impossible. Only Game of Thrones S1-4, The Wire, True Detective S1 cinematography

**Reality Check:** Most shows should score 7-8. If I'm handing out 9s freely, I'm being too generous.

## üìä CATEGORY PATTERNS

### Characters & Acting (20%)
**How to score:**
- **9.5-10** ‚Äî 6+ extraordinary characters (Breaking Bad, The Sopranos, Succession)
- **9.0** ‚Äî 4-5 deep characters, lead is transcendent (The Bear, The Americans)
- **8.5** ‚Äî 4-5 strong characters, lead excellent (Sh≈çgun, Slow Horses, Ripley)
- **8.0** ‚Äî Good ensemble, some standouts (Mad Men, Lost, Dark)
- **7.5** ‚Äî Functional, some memorable (Squid Game)
- **7.0** ‚Äî Lead carries thin supporting cast (Mare of Easttown, Pachinko)

**Common error:** Giving 9s to shows with ~4 good characters. That's 8.5 territory.

### World Building (15%)
**The encyclopedia test:** Could I write a wiki about this world?

- **10** ‚Äî Only The Wire, Game of Thrones (Baltimore institutions, Westeros history)
- **9.5** ‚Äî Nearly encyclopedic (Dark's time travel logic, Expanse's solar system politics)
- **9.0** ‚Äî Babylon Berlin, The Expanse
- **8.5** ‚Äî Deadwood, Boardwalk Empire, Rome, Andor, Sh≈çgun
- **8.0** ‚Äî Strong period work (Mad Men, The Crown, Narcos)
- **7.5** ‚Äî Specific but limited (Slow Horses, The Day of the Jackal)
- **6.5-7.0** ‚Äî Intimate dramas where world is backdrop (The Bear, Six Feet Under)
- **5.0** ‚Äî Generic/minimal

### Cinematography (10%)
**The frame test:** Could I identify this show from a single frame?

- **10** ‚Äî True Detective S1, Hannibal, Utopia UK (unmistakable visual identity)
- **9.5** ‚Äî The Knick, Mindhunter, Adolescence (director-driven masterpieces)
- **9.0** ‚Äî Breaking Bad, Better Call Saul, Mr. Robot, Babylon Berlin (distinctive, intentional)
- **8.5** ‚Äî Sh≈çgun, The Bear, Dark, Ripley (strong craft, recognizable)
- **8.0** ‚Äî Squid Game, The Queen's Gambit (stylish but not iconic)
- **7.5** ‚Äî Professional, effective, not distinctive

**Note:** The Sopranos, The Wire, The Shield score 6-7 intentionally‚Äîunglamorous realism is the point.

### Visual Spectacle (10%)
**Not cinematography ‚Äî what is shown, not how it's shot**

- **10** ‚Äî Foundation (galactic scale throughout)
- **9.5** ‚Äî Game of Thrones S1-4, House of the Dragon (dragons, battles, castles)
- **9.0** ‚Äî Babylon Berlin, Chernobyl, Band of Brothers, Sh≈çgun, Squid Game
- **8.5** ‚Äî Rome, Boardwalk Empire, The Expanse, Andor
- **8.0** ‚Äî The Queen's Gambit, Ripley, Mare of Easttown
- **7.5** ‚Äî Modest period work, some ambition
- **6.5** ‚Äî The Bear, The Americans (intentionally small scale)
- **5.0** ‚Äî Offices, suburbs, homes

### Conceptual Density (15%)
**How many college courses could be taught using this show?

- **10** ‚Äî The Wire (institutions, race, class, education, media, labor, politics), Angels in America
- **9.0** ‚Äî Game of Thrones, The Sopranos, Dark (multiple rich ideas from different angles)
- **8.5** ‚Äî Sh≈çgun, Andor
- **8.0** ‚Äî Breaking Bad, Better Call Saul, Mad Men, The Americans (focused but deep, 3-4 major themes)
- **7.5** ‚Äî Slow Horses, Squid Game, Pachinko (clear themes, explored well)
- **7.0** ‚Äî Succession (one central idea repeated), Mare of Easttown (familiar territory)
- **6.5** ‚Äî The Day of the Jackal (standard thriller themes)

**Key:** Ideas must be dramatized, not stated. Theme ‚â† density.

### Narrative Drive (15%)
**Do I need to watch the next episode, or can I wait?**

- **10** ‚Äî Breaking Bad, Chernobyl, Game of Thrones S1-4 (relentless momentum)
- **9.0-9.5** ‚Äî Better Call Saul, The Shield, 24, Beef, Severance, Squid Game (propulsive, hard to stop)
- **8.5** ‚Äî Sh≈çgun, Slow Horses, The Bear, The Queen's Gambit (compelling, some deliberate pacing)
- **8.0** ‚Äî The Wire, The Sopranos, Dark (rewarding but tests patience)
- **7.5** ‚Äî Ripley, Pachinko (deliberately slow, literary pacing)
- **6.0** ‚Äî Mad Men, Six Feet Under (glacial pace, seasons drag)
- **5.0** ‚Äî Lost (spinning wheels, frustrating)

---

### Narrative Resolution (15%)
**Did the show earn its ending? Did it close its loops?**

**THIS SEPARATES GOOD FROM GREAT**

- **10** ‚Äî Breaking Bad, Better Call Saul, The Americans, Six Feet Under, Chernobyl (perfect or near-perfect closure)
- **9.5** ‚Äî Dark (every thread connects, bootstrap paradox resolved)
- **9.0** ‚Äî The Queen's Gambit (complete self-contained arc)
- **8.5** ‚Äî The Sopranos, The Wire, Fargo
- **8.0** ‚Äî Mad Men, Ripley, Mare of Easttown (solid, most threads tied)
- **7.5** ‚Äî Sh≈çgun (strong season finale, opens next threads)
- **7.0** ‚Äî The Bear, Slow Horses, Squid Game (closes season loops, larger story open)
- **6.5-7.0** ‚Äî Pachinko, The Day of the Jackal (ongoing, incomplete but not frustrating)
- **5.0** ‚Äî Lost, The Handmaid's Tale (mystery boxes, abandoned threads, frustrating)

**Important:** Ambiguity is fine (The Sopranos = 9.0). Unresolved mess is not (Lost = 5.0).

## ‚ö†Ô∏è COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. **Too generous with 9s** ‚Äî 9s are elite tier. Most shows 7-8.
2. **Undervaluing Resolution** ‚Äî This is what makes shows great, not good.
3. **Overvaluing spectacle** ‚Äî Sh≈çgun isn't GoT scale. Intimate shows score lower on Spect but that's okay.
4. **Forgetting the encyclopedia test** ‚Äî World Building 9+ = encyclopedic (GoT, The Wire). Most shows 7-8.
5. **Confusing theme with density** ‚Äî One clear theme ‚â† high Conceptual Density.
6. **Rushing** ‚Äî Compare to anchors. Take time. Quality over speed.

## ‚úÖ SANITY CHECK QUESTIONS

Before finalizing a score:

1. **Would I rank this above [anchor at similar score]?**
   - If scoring 7.8: "Is this as good as Mad Men?"
   - If scoring 8.2: "Is this as good as The Expanse?"

2. **Am I being too generous?**
   - Too many 9s = recalibrate
   - Compare to existing shows at that score

3. **Does the Resolution score make sense?**
   - Complete story ‚Üí 8-9+
   - Ongoing/good season finale ‚Üí 7-7.5
   - Ongoing/concerning direction ‚Üí 6
   - Abandoned threads ‚Üí 5

4. **Would this feel right in the ranked list?**
   - Imagine inserting it. Does it fit?
