The Diplomat

What It Feels Like

Kate Wyler is a career diplomat. She's sent to the UK as ambassador during a crisis. She's not prepared for politics. Keri Russell is Kate: competent, overwhelmed, in over her head. Netflix political thriller: West Wing meets Homeland.

The 7-Dimension Analysis

Characters & Acting (8/10)

Keri Russell as Kate: intensity with humor. Rufus Sewell as her husband. David Gyasi, Allison Janney. The ensemble creates political world.

World Building (8/10)

US Embassy in London: diplomacy, politics, international crisis. The world is high-stakes international relations.

Cinematography (7/10)

Netflix production: London locations, embassies, the visual style of political thriller.

Visual Spectacle (3/10)

Politics, meetings, crisis rooms. The spectacle is tension, not action.

Conceptual Density (8/10)

Diplomacy vs. politics, marriage under pressure, what competence means. The show explores political machinery.

Narrative Drive (8/10)

Crisis response, political survival, marriage survival. The drive is high-stakes drama.

Narrative Path & Resolution (5/10)

Ongoing. Season 1 cliffhanger. The story continues. No resolution yet.
