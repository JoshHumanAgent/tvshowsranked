WeCrashed

2022 • Apple TV+ • 8 Episodes

Score: 7.10
Drama, Business

What It Feels Like

Adam Neumann built WeWork into a $47 billion company. Then it collapsed. Jared Leto and Anne Hathaway play the Neumanns: charisma, delusion, and the rise and fall of the startup era. Real-life hubris as prestige TV.

The 7-Dimension Analysis

Characters & Acting (8/10)

Jared Leto as Adam: commitment to physical transformation. Anne Hathaway as Rebekah: the wife who enabled. The performances capture the delusion.

World Building (7/10)

WeWork: the offices, the culture, the KPI. The world is startup era excess rendered specific.

Cinematography (7/10)

Apple TV+ production: the offices, the excess, the visual language of tech money.

Visual Spectacle (3/10)

Luxury offices, private jets. The spectacle is money made visible.

Conceptual Density (8/10)

Hype economy, charismatic leadership, the tech bubble. The show explores an era through one company.

Narrative Drive (8/10)

Rise and fall. Each episode advances toward collapse. The structure is tragedy foretold.

Narrative Path & Resolution (7/10)

Complete miniseries. The fall is shown; the aftermath is aftermath. Real story, limited series.
