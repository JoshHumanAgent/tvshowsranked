## What It Feels Like to Watch

Bloodline is a slow-burn thriller about the Rayburn family, whose seemingly perfect life on the Florida Keys is destroyed when their estranged black sheep brother returns home.

You watch Bloodline and feel the tension building. The series takes its time, letting the dread accumulate. Every family dinner is loaded with unspoken resentments. Every smile hides a secret.
