The O.C.

2003 - 2007 • Fox • 92 Episodes

Score: 7.05
Drama, Teen

What It Feels Like

Ryan is a kid from Chino. The Cohens take him in. Now he lives in Newport Beach. Josh Schwartz's teen drama: rich kids, poor kid, California. Seth Cohen is forever. The show defined 2000s teen television.

The 7-Dimension Analysis

Characters & Acting (8/10)

Ben McKenzie as Ryan: silent intensity. Adam Brody as Seth: the character every nerd wanted to be. Mischa Barton, Rachel Bilson. The cast defined 2000s teen drama.

World Building (8/10)

Newport Beach: the mansions, the beach, the high school, the Bait Shop. The world is California dream made television.

Cinematography (5/10)

2000s network TV: bright, California sun, the visual style of teen soap.

Visual Spectacle (2/10)

California wealth. The spectacle is lifestyle. Low action spectacle.

Conceptual Density (6/10)

Class, family, adolescence. The show explored teen issues with sincerity and humor.

Narrative Drive (7/10)

Soapy drama: relationships, family conflicts, high school. The drive is teen melodrama.

Narrative Path & Resolution (6/10)

Four seasons. Quality declined after season 1. The finale is satisfying but the show peaked early.
