What It Feels Like

Cassandra wakes up in Bangkok with a dead man beside her. She's hungover. She doesn't remember. She runs. Kaley Cuoco is Cassie: messy, alcoholic, and now a suspect. HBO's thriller-comedy: bright colors, dark subject, addiction as mystery.
