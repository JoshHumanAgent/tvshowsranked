Resurrection (2014)

2014 - 2015 • ABC • 21 Episodes

Score: 6.95

What It Feels Like

The dead return to Arcadia, Missouri. Not as zombies—as themselves. They haven't aged. They don't know they died. The Returned's American adaptation: what if the dead came back? Not horror, but mystery and family drama.

The 7-Dimension Analysis

Characters & Acting (7/10)

Omar Epps, Frances Fisher, Kurtwood Smith. The returning dead and their families. Strong ensemble.

World Building (7/10)

Arcadia: small town America confronting impossible returns. The world is mystery and grief.

Cinematography (6/10)

ABC production: small town mystery, the visual language of supernatural drama.

Visual Spectacle (3/10)

The spectacle is the impossible return. No action—emotional impact.

Conceptual Density (7/10)

Grief, second chances, whether we'd want the dead back. The show explores loss through supernatural premise.

Narrative Drive (7/10)

Why are they back? What does it mean? The mystery drives the drama.

Narrative Path & Resolution (5/10)

Two seasons, cancelled. The mystery was never fully resolved. A tragedy of cancellation.
