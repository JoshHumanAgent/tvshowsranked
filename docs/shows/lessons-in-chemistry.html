What It Feels Like

Elizabeth Zott is a chemist in the 1960s. She's brilliant. The men around her won't let her be a scientist. So she becomes a cooking show host—and teaches chemistry through recipes. Brie Larson in Bonnie Garmus's adaptation: feminist period drama.
