Only Murders in the Building

2021 - Present • Hulu • Ongoing

Score: 7.55

What It Feels Like

Three strangers love the same true crime podcast. Then there's a murder in their building. They decide to solve it and start a podcast. Steve Martin, Martin Short, and Selena Gomez solve mysteries. A love letter to true crime obsession.

The 7-Dimension Analysis

Characters & Acting (9/10)

Steve Martin as Charles: the aging actor. Martin Short as Oliver: the Broadway director. Selena Gomez as Mabel: the mysterious young woman. The trio's chemistry carries the show.

World Building (8/10)

The Arconia: the Upper West Side apartment building with secrets. The world is cozy mystery—rich people, secrets, elevators.

Cinematography (7/10)

Hulu production: the building's interiors, New York cityscapes, the podcast aesthetic. Stylish but not showy.

Visual Spectacle (4/10)

The spectacle is New York: the building, the Broadway world. Low spectacle by design—it's a cozy mystery.

Conceptual Density (7/10)

True crime obsession, loneliness in cities, found friends, performance vs. reality. The show explores these through comedy-mystery.

Narrative Drive (8/10)

Each season a new murder. The podcast investigation structure. The drive is whodunit with laughs.

Narrative Path & Resolution (5/10)

Ongoing. Each season resolves its murder. No end in sight—this is the format.
