Ozark

Netflix Crime Thriller | 2017-2022 | 4 Seasons | 44 Episodes
Created by Bill Dubuque & Mark Williams
Financial advisor Marty Byrde makes a desperate decision to uproot his family from Chicago and relocate to the Missouri Ozarks after a money laundering scheme goes disastrously wrong. Forced to launder millions for a deadly Mexican drug cartel, Marty must navigate treacherous local politics, dangerous criminals, and his own family's moral erosion while trying to keep everyone alive.
Starring: Jason Bateman, Laura Linney, Julia Garner, Tom Pelphrey, Sofia Hublitz, Skylar Gaertner
