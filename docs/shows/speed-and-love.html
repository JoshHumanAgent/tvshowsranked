What It Feels Like

Formula racing romance with engineering genius meets reckless driver. Lu Yao is a prodigy engineer who understands cars better than people; Jiang Chen is the reckless driver who needs her expertise. Speed and Love is Chinese sports-romance: fast cars, competitive racing, and the chemistry between engineer and driver. The racing sequences are surprisingly authentic—motor sports production values. The romance follows sports-drama beats: rivals become partners, attraction under pressure, team dynamics. Chinese television at its most competent: formulaic but executed well.
