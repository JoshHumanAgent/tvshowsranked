What It Feels Like to Watch

Evil asks questions The Good Wife couldn't: Is evil real? Kristen Bouchard is a forensic psychologist investigating strange phenomena for the Catholic Church. Some cases are hoaxes. Some are mental illness. Some... are something else.

The King siblings perfected the case-of-the-week format. Each episode presents genuine ambiguity about what's happening. The show never commits to whether demons exist, which makes it more terrifying.

Katja Herbers and Mike Colter have magnetic chemistry. Michael Emerson's Leland Townsend is nightmare fuel: pure, gleeful malevolence.
