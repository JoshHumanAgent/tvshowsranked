Felicity

1998 - 2002 • The WB • 84 Episodes

Score: 7.10
Drama, College

What It Feels Like

Felicity follows Ben to college. She barely knows him. She made a decision based on a note in his yearbook. Keri Russell is Felicity: making impulsive choices and living with them. JJ Abrams and Matt Reeves created the college drama: growing up happens after high school.

The 7-Dimension Analysis

Characters & Acting (8/10)

Keri Russell as Felicity: earnest, searching, real. Scott Speedman as Ben, Scott Foley as Noel. The love triangle that mattered.

World Building (7/10)

NYU (fictional as "UNY"): the dorms, the city, college life. The world is young adulthood in New York.

Cinematography (6/10)

90s WB production: New York City, college dorms, the visual style of intimate drama.

Visual Spectacle (1/10)

No spectacle. This is college students figuring out life. The spectacle is growth.

Conceptual Density (8/10)

Identity, choices, who we become in late adolescence. The show explored coming of age with unusual depth.

Narrative Drive (7/10)

Ben vs. Noel. Finding yourself. College drama structure with real stakes.

Narrative Path & Resolution (8/10)

Four seasons, complete. The infamous time travel ending divides fans but provides closure. Unique conclusion.
