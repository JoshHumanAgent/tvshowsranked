Castle Rock

2018 - 2019 • Hulu • 20 Episodes

Score: 7.20
Horror, Mystery

What It Feels Like

Castle Rock is the Stephen King universe town. Shawshank Prison is here. So is Henry Deaver, a death-row attorney. Season 1 is about a prisoner and the house at the center of everything. Season 2 is "Misery" prequel. The show connects King mythology.

The 7-Dimension Analysis

Characters & Acting (8/10)

André Holland, Sissy Spacek, Bill Skarsgård. Season 2 adds Lizzy Caplan as Annie Wilkes. The performances serve the King homage.

World Building (9/10)

Castle Rock: Shawshank, the Kid, the town's dark history. The world is Stephen King Maine made coherent.

Cinematography (8/10)

Hulu production: Maine's forests, the prison, the horrors. Atmospheric King adaptation.

Visual Spectacle (6/10)

King horror moments: the Kid, Shawshank, Annie's origin. Moderate spectacle.

Conceptual Density (8/10)

Fate, evil, the nature of the Kid, whether we escape our pasts. King themes explored.

Narrative Drive (7/10)

Season 1: the mystery of the Kid. Season 2: Annie before Lisey's Story. The drive is King mythology.

Narrative Path & Resolution (6/10)

Two seasons, cancelled. Some mysteries unresolved. The King-verse continues without this show.
