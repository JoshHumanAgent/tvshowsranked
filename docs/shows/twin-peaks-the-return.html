What It Feels Like to Watch

25 years later, Lynch returned. 18 hours of pure Lynchian dream logic—Cooper trapped, doppelgängers loose, atomic bombs, talking electricity. Episode 8 is the most experimental hour in television history.

Not for everyone. The pace is glacial, the logic dream, the payoff ambiguous. But for those who surrender, it's art.
