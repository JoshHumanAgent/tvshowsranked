What It Feels Like

Wednesday Addams goes to Nevermore Academy. She's a detective—solving mysteries, avoiding feelings, being brilliantly deadpan. Tim Burton directs Jenna Ortega: goth girl as hero, mystery as teen drama.

The 7-Dimension Analysis

Characters & Acting (8/10)

Jenna Ortega as Wednesday: deadpan perfection, dance viral everywhere. Catherine Zeta-Jones as Morticia. The cast supports Wednesday's journey.

World Building (8/10)

Nevermore Academy: the school for supernatural outcasts. The town, the woods, the mystery. The Addams Family universe expanded.

Cinematography (8/10)

Netflix/Tim Burton: Gothic production design, Wednesday's aesthetic. The style is Burton-dark-teen.

Visual Spectacle (7/10)

The school, the mystery, Wednesday's style, the dance. Spectacle through aesthetic.

Conceptual Density (6/10)

Identity, outsiderness, family legacy, being different. Standard teen themes through goth lens.

Narrative Drive (7/10)

The mystery of Nevermore; Wednesday's cold case. Teen detective structure.

Narrative Path & Resolution (5/10)

Ongoing. Season 1 solves its mystery; more planned. Wednesday's story continues.
