What It Feels Like to Watch

AMC's Halt and Catch Fire is Mad Men for the PC revolution. Lee Pace's Joe MacMillan is the visionary sociopath; Scoot McNairy's Gordon Clark is the engineer family man; Mackenzie Davis' Cameron Howe is the punk programmer. Together they build IBM clones, then websites, then the future.

Four seasons trace the personal computer revolution through three damaged people. The show grows from prestige imitation into something intimate and affecting.
