What It Feels Like to Watch

Charlie Brooker's anthology of tech anxiety. Each episode is a different nightmare—social media ratings, consciousness upload, digital afterlife, political porn. The best episodes ("White Bear," "San Junipero," "USS Callister") are television events.

The premise limits the show: anthology format means no continuity, no cumulative impact. But at its best, it's the Twilight Zone for the social media age.
