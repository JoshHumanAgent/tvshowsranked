Community

2009 - 2015 • NBC/Yahoo • 110 Episodes

Score: 7.65
Comedy, Meta

What It Feels Like

Jeff Winger is a disbarred lawyer who enrolls at Greendale Community College to get an easy degree. He meets a study group. Dan Harmon created the most meta sitcom: parody of genres, layers of irony, the show that keeps commenting on itself.

The 7-Dimension Analysis

Characters & Acting (9/10)

Joel McHale as Jeff: narcissist with heart. Gillian Jacobs as Britta: the activist who doesn't know anything. Donald Glover as Troy, Danny Pudi as Abed. The ensemble created comedy gold.

World Building (8/10)

Greendale: the community college as absurd universe, Dean Pelton, Chang, the paintball episodes. The world is anything-parody.

Cinematography (7/10)

Network sitcom that becomes film parody. Each genre episode has its style.

Visual Spectacle (5/10)

The spectacle is parody: action movies, documentaries, claymation. The "spectacle" is the concept.

Conceptual Density (9/10)

Meta-humor, pop culture references, found family through college, redemption arcs. The show is about itself.

Narrative Drive (7/10)

Jeff's degree; study group bonds. Episodic parodies with serialized character growth.

Narrative Path & Resolution (7/10)

Six seasons and a movie (eventually). The Yahoo! season ends the story. A meta ending for a meta show.
