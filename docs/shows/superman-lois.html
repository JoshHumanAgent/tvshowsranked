Superman & Lois

2021 - Present • The CW • Ongoing

Score: 7.30
Superhero, Family

What It Feels Like

Superman is 40. He has two teenage sons. One might have powers. He moved the family to Smallville. Tyler Hoechlin is Clark Kent: the dad struggling with parenthood while saving the world. The CW superhero show that prioritized family over action.

The 7-Dimension Analysis

Characters & Acting (8/10)

Tyler Hoechlin as Superman: warmth and strength. Elizabeth Tulloch as Lois Lane. The sons create family stakes. The best Superman since Reeve.

World Building (7/10)

>p>Smallville restored: the farm, the community, the Daily Planet. The world is Superman as family man.

Cinematography (7/10)

CW production: cinematic for TV, Smallville locations, the visual language of grounded superhero.

Visual Spectacle (6/10)

Superman action, but limited. The spectacle is secondary to family drama.

Conceptual Density (8/10)

>p>Parenthood, legacy, being Superman while being human. The show explores responsibility through family.

Narrative Drive (7/10)

>p>Superman saves the day; Clark raises the kids. Balanced narrative. The drive is the double life.

Narrative Path & Resolution (5/10)

>p>Ongoing. Four seasons. The family story continues.
