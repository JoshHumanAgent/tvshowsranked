What It Feels Like

Based on the life of Rigoberto Urán, Colombian cyclist who survived tragedy and became a professional champion. Rigo is his nicknamed childhood; the show follows his journey from humble beginnings through family loss to international cycling. Colombian television biopic: authentic locations, real-life sports drama, family struggles, and the triumph of representing Colombia on the world stage. The cycling sequences are filmed at Tour de France quality. The personal story is emotional—Uran's father died in a cycling accident; Rigo could have quit but chose to ride. The show is pride and survival.
