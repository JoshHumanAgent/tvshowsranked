Dawson's Creek

1998 - 2003 • The WB • 128 Episodes

Score: 6.90
Drama, Teen

What It Feels Like

Dawson, Joey, Pacey, Jen: four teenagers in Capeside, Massachusetts. Kevin Williamson created the teen drama with vocabulary beyond their years. Dawson loves Joey. Joey loves Dawson. Then Pacey loves Joey. The love triangle that defined late-90s teen television.

The 7-Dimension Analysis

Characters & Acting (7/10)

James Van Der Beek, Katie Holmes, Joshua Jackson, Michelle Williams. The cast launched careers. The dialogue was pretentious but the emotions were real.

World Building (7/10)

Capeside: small town New England, the creek, the bedroom window. The world is idyllic teen fantasy.

Cinematography (5/10)

90s WB production: soft lighting, teen soap aesthetics. Dated but charming.

Visual Spectacle (1/10)

No spectacle. Teen drama in small town. The spectacle is emotion.

Conceptual Density (7/10)

Coming of age, first love, ambition vs. staying home. The show explored teen intellect through melodrama.

Narrative Drive (7/10)

The love triangle, growing up, leaving home. Teen soap structure with emotional stakes.

Narrative Path & Resolution (7/10)

Six seasons, complete. The love triangle resolves. The finale delivers. Kevin Williamson's teen drama conclusion.
